The program

Eight units. A complete foundation in reading.

Each unit builds on the last — from hearing sounds to reading fluently, spelling confidently, and writing independently.

01
Unit 01 · Sessions 1–10

Listening, Sounds & Phonological Awareness

Sessions
Sessions 1–3Listening & Rhyming
Sessions 4–7Syllables & Sound Counting
Sessions 8–10Beginning, Middle & End Sounds
After this unit, your child can:
✓Identify first, last, and middle sounds in a word
✓Tell you whether two words start with the same sound
✓Produce at least 2 rhymes for a given word
✓Clap out sounds in a word
02
Unit 02 · Sessions 11–20

Letter Recognition & Letter Sounds

Sessions
Sessions 11–13Vowels — a, e, i, o, u (Short vs Long)
Sessions 14–17Consonants b–m
Sessions 18–20Consonants n–z
After this unit, your child can:
✓Name and form all 26 letters with their sounds
✓Match pictures to their beginning sound
✓Differentiate between short and long vowel sounds
03
Unit 03 · Sessions 21–32

CVC Words, Blending & Word Families

Sessions
Sessions 21–24Blending CVC Words
Sessions 25–27Short -a and short -i word families
Sessions 28–30Short -o, -u, -e
Sessions 31–32High Frequency Words & Sentence Reading
After this unit, your child can:
✓Read and spell 50+ CVC words independently
✓Use word families to read unfamiliar words
✓Read 20 highly used sight words
✓Read simple 3–4 word sentences
✓Spell CVC words correctly from dictation
04
Unit 04 · Sessions 33–43

Digraphs & Consonant Blends

Sessions
Sessions 33–36Digraphs — sh, ch, th
Sessions 37–40R, L, S Blends
Session 41End Blends
Sessions 42–433-letter Blends
After this unit, your child can:
✓Read and spell words with initial and final blends
✓Blend high-frequency words with digraphs
✓Build better vocabulary through pattern recognition
05
Unit 05 · Sessions 44–50

Magic E & Long Vowel Sounds

Sessions
Sessions 44–45Magic E & Long Vowel Introduction
Session 46Long A sounds
Session 47Long E sounds
Session 48Long I sounds
Session 49Long O sounds
Session 50Long U sounds
After this unit, your child can:
✓Understand how magic e changes a vowel sound
✓Read and use all long vowel patterns
✓Use high-frequency words with long vowel sounds
06
Unit 06 · Sessions 51–57

R-Controlled Vowels & Diphthongs

Sessions
Sessions 51–52R-controlled Vowels — ar, er, ir, or, ur
Sessions 53–54Tricky Long Vowel Teams — igh, ei, oe
Sessions 56–57Diphthongs — au, aw, oi/oy, ou/ow
After this unit, your child can:
✓Read all major vowel teams accurately
✓Use igh, oi/oy, ou/ow correctly in spelling
✓Show fluency when reading paragraphs
✓Build vocabulary using unit patterns
07
Unit 07 · Sessions 58–69

Spelling Rules

Sessions
Sessions 58–59Silent Letters — kn, wr, gn, mb
Session 60Soft vs Hard C
Session 61Soft vs Hard G
Sessions 62–63Floss Rule — ff, ll, ss, zz
Sessions 64–65Doubling Rule (e.g. running)
Sessions 66–67Drop-E Rule (e.g. making)
Sessions 68–69Y-to-I Rule (e.g. happier)
After this unit, your child can:
✓Apply the doubling rule confidently
✓Apply the drop-e rule when adding suffixes
✓Distinguish soft and hard C & G sounds
✓Spell silent letter words correctly
✓Know and use more sight words
08
Unit 08 · Sessions 70–80

Fluency, Comprehension & Punctuation

Sessions
Sessions 70–72Reading Decodable Books
Sessions 73–76Reading Comprehension
Session 77Capital Letters, Full Stop & Question Mark
Sessions 78–80Writing with Dictation
After this unit, your child can:
✓Read a Level 1 book from start to finish
✓Understand and explain what they've read
✓Use full stops, capitals & question marks correctly
✓Explain word meanings using context clues
✓Write a sentence to dictation independently
